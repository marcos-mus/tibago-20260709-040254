Healthcare Access Challenges in Africa

Many people in Africa face limited access to healthcare due to distance from medical centers, shortage of specialists, high travel costs, and long waiting times.

  • Geographical barriers limit access to quality healthcare.
  • Shortage of medical specialists in many regions.
  • High costs associated with traveling to healthcare facilities.
  • Long delays and waiting times for consultations.

Our Telemedicine Solution

TibaGo connects patients with certified health professionals remotely through mobile and web platforms, enabling medical consultations, prescriptions, and personalized follow-up without the need to travel.

  • Remote consultations via mobile and web.
  • Access to certified medical professionals anytime, anywhere.
  • Personalized health monitoring and follow-up.
  • Reduced costs and waiting times for patients.
